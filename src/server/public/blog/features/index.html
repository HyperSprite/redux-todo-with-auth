Welcome to A Race athlete

Getting started is easy. All you need to do is click the Connect with STRAVA button in the upper right corner. You will be redirected to Strava to sign in and they will return you, logged in and ready to go.

Find any activity you’ve ever done on Strava using keywords, location, date, and a host of other filters.

  • Text Search can use either keywords or precision text and results can be sorted on key metrics.
  • Location Search lets you drop a pin on a map and return Activities within a specified Max Distance (default is 50 miles), sorted by their proximity to your pin.

This is your week at a glance with metrics accumulation, gear totals, and activity data breakdowns.

  • Accumulation Charts stacks the days total on top of the past week for key metrics such as Distance, Elevation, and Time, as well as extended data such as TSS, Suffer Score, Kilojoules, and Calories.
  • Gear breakdown shows you the accumulation of key metrics by gear.
  • Comprehensive data breakdown for each activity.

A community curated list of events that helps you organize, plan and track your season.

  • Find or create events you are interested in and favorite them. Then filter your list on your favorites and you have a handy season schedule.
  • Events have a common format so it is easy to find the information quickly, as well as links to the event organizers home page.
  • Extended information such as weather, time zone, and starting elevation which are for planning.

Every cyclist who has ever gone uphill knows it is all about power to weight ratio. You can find your power to weight on Strava if you look at your fitness chart and it only tells you what it is today. We track power and weight so you can see how they change over time and plot it to a graph for easy reference.

Using your Strava FTP and Location, we generate your personalized Power at Altitude chart with power zones for both acclimated and non-acclimated power.
