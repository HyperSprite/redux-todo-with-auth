Power and Weight

When it comes to Climbing, it is all about Power and Weight, and well, Endurance, but this page focuses on Power and Weight.

At this time, Strava lets you enter your current FTP and Weight, but it only keeps this as a single value and does not save a history. Update your Strava numbers as necessary and A Race athlete will keep track of this for you on this page. We will also calculate and track your Watts per Kilogram while we are at it.

If you would like to focus in on a smaller range of data, use the slider below the charts.
