A Race athlete Blog

Getting started is easy. All you need to do is click the Connect with STRAVA button in the upper right corner. You will be redirected to Strava to sign in and they will return you, logged in and ready to go.

Once inside, use the icon in the upper left to open the Menu Drawer.

Advanced features, such as adding new events, also requires membership to the A Race athlete Strava club (here).

This app came about because I wanted to use it. It is a labor of love and suffering, much like cycling.
